The Pose App Structure (Main Tabs)

The Tweet tab is for text-based and mixed-media content.

  • Allows creators and users to build influence through ideas and conversations

3. Pose Tab (Long Videos, Movies, and Books)

The Pose tab is for long-form and premium content.

  • Shows videos from 2 minutes upward
  • Includes free and paid videos
  • Supports movies, series, documentaries, and tutorials
  • Contains a digital books section
  • Users can read books for free or pay to access premium content

This tab is where serious content creators, filmmakers, and educators earn long-term income.
